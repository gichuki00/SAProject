Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions about Recipe Finder

How do I submit my own recipes?

We love featuring recipes from our community! To submit your recipe, sign up for an account and click the "Submit Recipe" button in your dashboard. Our team will review your submission and get back to you within 3-5 business days.

Can I save recipes for later?

Yes! Once you create an account, you can save any recipe to your personal collection by clicking the heart icon on recipe cards. You can organize your saved recipes into custom categories for easy access.

Are the recipes tested and reliable?

Absolutely! All recipes on Recipe Finder are tested by our team of professional chefs and home cooks. We provide detailed instructions, cooking times, and tips to ensure your dishes turn out perfectly every time.

Do you offer mobile apps?

Yes! Recipe Finder is available on both iOS and Android. Download our mobile app to access recipes offline, create shopping lists, and get notifications when new recipes are added.

How do I report an issue with a recipe?

If you encounter any issues with a recipe, please use the "Report Issue" button on the recipe page or contact us directly at support@recipefinder.com. We appreciate your feedback and will address any problems promptly.
